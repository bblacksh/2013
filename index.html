I'm going to do something a little different here, just for fun and to mix it up. 30 artists that meant a lot to me this year, and 2 songs from each of them. In random order.

Julianna Barwick

Those first cold, fragile breaths. Change is hard sometimes, disorienting, difficult to accept, but The Harbinger is here to help. A repetitive meditative process, slowly working at it, getting used to it, on your own at first, but a chorus of voices and strings helps you keep moving, all building to that supremely cathartic "AHHHHH!" No song moved me more than this one this year.

Eleanor Friedberger

Eleanor captures the confused joyful feeling of being in love followed by the confusion of heartbreak. Personal Record is perhaps my favorite of this year, with witty but poignant songwriting throughout, beautifully delivered by Elanor's charismatic voice.
